    Polynomial Expressions

    Grade 9 Unit 1 on polynomial expressions introduces students to the basics of algebraic expressions, focusing on understanding terms, coefficients, and constants. Students learn to simplify, add, subtract, and expand polynomial expressions, laying the foundation for more complex algebraic operations.

  • Unit 2

    Algebra

    Grade 9 Unit 2 on algebra introduces students to foundational algebraic concepts, including variables, expressions, and equations. Students learn to solve simple linear equations, understand the order of operations, and apply algebraic reasoning to solve real-world problems.

  • Unit 3

    Data Management

    Grade 9 Unit 3 on data management teaches students how to collect, organize, and interpret data using various methods such as tables, graphs, and charts. Students explore measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode) and learn to analyze trends to make informed conclusions.

  • Unit 4

    Slope

    Grade 9 Unit 4 on slope introduces students to the concept of slope as a measure of the steepness of a line, calculated as the ratio of the rise over run between two points. Students learn to determine and interpret slope in various contexts, including graphing linear relationships and understanding positive, negative, zero, and undefined slopes.

  • Unit 5

    Equation of a Line

    Grade 9 Unit 5 on the equation of a line teaches students how to write and interpret linear equations in different forms, such as slope-intercept form (y = mx + b) and standard form (Ax + By = C). Students learn to use these equations to graph lines, identify slope and y-intercept, and analyze the relationships between variables in linear contexts.

  • Unit 6

    Geometric Relationships

    Grade 9 Unit 6 on geometric relationships introduces students to the properties and classifications of angles, triangles, and other polygons. Students explore concepts such as congruence, similarity, and the Pythagorean theorem to solve problems involving side lengths and angle measures in various geometric figures.

  • Unit 7

    3D Geometry

    Grade 9 Unit 7 on 3D geometry teaches students to analyze and describe three-dimensional shapes, including prisms, cylinders, pyramids, and cones. Students learn to calculate surface area and volume, applying these concepts to solve real-world problems involving space and capacity.

  • Unit 8

    Optimization

    Grade 9 Unit 8 on optimization introduces students to strategies for finding the most efficient solutions to problems involving area, perimeter, surface area, and volume. Students learn to apply mathematical reasoning to maximize or minimize quantities in various real-life contexts.
